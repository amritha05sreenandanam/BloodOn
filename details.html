Our Mission

The Blood Donation Alert System is a social service–driven web platform created to bridge the gap between urgent medical needs and compassionate individuals willing to help. We connect voluntary blood donors with hospitals and patients in need, ensuring timely access to life-saving blood donations.

Our platform transforms moments of crisis into opportunities for collective care, reducing uncertainty and delay while offering reassurance, responsiveness, and hope when it matters most.

How It Works

For Donors:

  1. Click on "Donor Login" from the home page
  2. Fill in your registration form with:
    • Your full name
    • Blood group
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Location (city/district)
  3. Click "Confirm Registration" to join our network
  4. You'll receive alerts via email and WhatsApp when there's a matching blood request in your area
  5. Contact the hospital directly using the provided information if you can help

For Hospitals/Requesters:

  1. Click on "Requester Login" from the home page
  2. Fill in the blood request form with:
    • Hospital name and contact details
    • Hospital location
    • Required blood group
    • Optional patient details
  3. Click "Submit Request"
  4. Our system automatically finds compatible donors based on blood type and location
  5. Donors receive notifications and can contact you directly
  6. View the donor match page to see how many donors were notified

Blood Compatibility

Our system automatically matches blood requests with compatible donors based on medical compatibility:

  • A+ can receive from: A+, A-, O+, O-
  • A- can receive from: A-, O-
  • B+ can receive from: B+, B-, O+, O-
  • B- can receive from: B-, O-
  • AB+ can receive from: All blood types (Universal Recipient)
  • AB- can receive from: A-, B-, AB-, O-
  • O+ can receive from: O+, O-
  • O- can receive from: O- (Universal Donor)

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Donor personal information is never displayed publicly. Only hospitals receive your contact details when you respond to a request. Your data is securely stored and used solely for the purpose of connecting you with blood requests.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a donor?
Click on "Donor Login" from the home page, fill in the registration form with your details, and click "Confirm Registration". You'll be added to our network and will receive alerts for matching blood requests.
Will my personal information be shared publicly?
No, your personal information is never displayed publicly. Only hospitals receive your contact details when there's a matching blood request, and only if you choose to respond.
How quickly will I receive alerts?
Alerts are sent immediately when a matching blood request is submitted. You'll receive notifications via both email and WhatsApp for faster response.
Can I update my information after registration?
Currently, you would need to register again with updated information. We're working on adding an update feature in future versions.
What if I'm not available when I receive an alert?
That's completely fine! Receiving an alert doesn't obligate you to donate. Only respond if you're available and willing to help. There are usually multiple donors notified for each request.
How does the location matching work?
Our system prioritizes donors in the same or nearby locations to the hospital. Donors in the same city or district are notified first, followed by those in nearby areas if needed.
Is there a cost to use this service?
No, this is a completely free service for both donors and hospitals. Our mission is to save lives, not to make a profit.
Can hospitals see all donor information?
No, hospitals only receive your contact information (email and phone) when you're matched with a request. They don't have access to browse all donors or see your full profile.
